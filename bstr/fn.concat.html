Concatenate the elements given by the iterator together into a single Vec<u8>.

The elements may be any type that can be cheaply converted into an &[u8]. This includes, but is not limited to, &str, &BStr and &[u8] itself.

§Examples

Basic usage:

use bstr;

let s = bstr::concat(&["foo", "bar", "baz"]);
assert_eq!(s, "foobarbaz".as_bytes());
